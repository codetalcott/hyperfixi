The htmx-v4 bundle's @hyperfixi/reactivity provides two-way bind for form elements. bind $color to me auto-detects the readable property (value for inputs) and wires the bind in both directions. The explicit-property form bind $x to me's prop is also supported; chained access (me's style.backgroundColor) is deferred — the swatch below uses hx-live instead for a reactive style update.

bind $color to me on a <input type="color"> (auto-detects value)
Same auto-detect bind on a second input — both stay in sync via $color
hx-live="set my @style to ..." — reactive style update

Try editing the text input directly (paste red, #0af, rebeccapurple) — the picker follows because both are bound to $color. The swatch follows because its hx-live body reads $color and the reactivity graph notifies it on every change.
