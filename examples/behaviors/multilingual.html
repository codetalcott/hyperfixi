Author behaviors in your language

The curated behaviors are defined in pure hyperscript, so their logic isn't English-only. A behavior body like on click toggle .active translates — with grammar transformation (SVO / SOV / VSO word order) — into any of 24 languages, and round-trips back to the same intent. This is what the imperative-JS detour threw away: you can't translate a JavaScript installer, but you can translate hyperscript. Below, each curated behavior's core handler is translated live by the LokaScriptSemantic engine.
