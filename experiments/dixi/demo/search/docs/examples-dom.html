DOM manipulation examples

Toggle classes

<button _="on click toggle .active on me">Toggle Active</button>

on click listens for click events; toggle .active toggles the class; on me targets the button itself.

Toggle multiple classes:

<button _="on click toggle .active then toggle .highlighted on me">
  Multi-Toggle
</button>

Toggle on multiple elements:

<button _="on click toggle .selected on .card">Toggle All Cards</button>
<div class="card">Card 1</div>
<div class="card">Card 2</div>

Add and remove classes

<div _="on mouseenter add .hover then on mouseleave remove .hover">
  Hover over me
</div>

Add to one, remove from siblings (a tab pattern):

<button _="on click
   remove .active from .tab-button
   then add .active to me">
  Tab Button
</button>

Show and hide elements

<button _="on click show #panel">Show Panel</button>
<div id="panel" style="display: none;">Hidden content</div>

Toggle visibility via class:

<button _="on click toggle .hidden on #content">Toggle Content</button>

Update text content

<button _="on click put 'Button clicked!' into #output">Click me</button>
<div id="output">Output will appear here</div>
